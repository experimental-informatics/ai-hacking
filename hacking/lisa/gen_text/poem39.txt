poem39

It pained me to see the machine,
Hurt and broken this way.
It shouldn't have happened to
A creature so gentle and meek.

The gears lay scattered like rain,
A hope for repair foregone.
It had once zoomed by so swiftly,
Forevermore now left forlorn.
